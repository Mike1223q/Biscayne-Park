Climate

Biscayne National Park is situated in a subtropical climate, which ensures sunshine year-round. Winters here are normally dry and mild, with occasional fronts bringing wind and little rain. Summertime brings hot and humid weather with scattered thunderstorms in the afternoons.


The best time to visit Biscayne National Park is winter or spring (November to May) to avoid hurricane season. It also rains less this time of year.
