Plants

Hundreds of different plant species can be found in the park including plants that grow under water such as seagrasses. Some of the most interesting plants are the mangroves and one of the longest continuous stretches of mangroves left on Florida's East coast is protected at Biscayne


The park is home to many threatened and endangered species including the West Indian manatee, eastern indigo snake, piping plover, American crocodile, peregrine falcon, Schaus' swallowtail butterfly, least tern, and 5 species of sea turtle.
